    Nicotine strength guide

    LiQuid shortfill e-liquids give you the most bang for your buck, allowing you to buy larger volumes of 0mg vape juice in a 120ml bottle, short filled to 100ml to allow for the addition of up to two 10ml Nic Shots for custom nicotine strength creation.

    80/20 HVG e-liquids are better suited for use with high power sub-ohm vaping devices, that use coils with a rating of 0.9ohm or below.

    1 x nicotine free 100ml shortfill + 1 x 18mg-20mg 10ml Nic Shot = 110ml 1.5mg approx.

    1 x nicotine free 100ml shortfill + 2 x 18mg-20mg 10ml Nic Shot = 120ml 3mg approx.

    After adding Nic Shots, shake and leave to stand for at least 5 minutes for best performance.
